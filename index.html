Game Instructions
This is a puzzle solving type of game. You will solve different puzzles using Data Encryption. To finish the game, you have to complete all objectives. The only way to lose this game is to press the give up button on the side.
Your menu bar has 4 options you can use:
ITEMS - in the game, you'll encounter items which you can use. You can view those items here
OBJECTIVES - you can view your objectives again here.
NOTEBOOK - you can view all the previous notes here about Data Encryption.
GIVE UP - this is the only way to lose the game. Do not press this if you intend to finish the game!
